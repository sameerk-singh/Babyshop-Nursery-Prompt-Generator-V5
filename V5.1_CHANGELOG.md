# V5.1 Prompter Changelog

Source of changes: feedback from the 273-image batch run (Nursery_Batch_Feedback.pdf).
All edits are in-code (`index.html`). Supabase schema is untouched.

---

## Shot structure

**Shot 7 (ARCHITECTURAL CONTEXT) removed from STANDARD and ADJUSTABLE tiers.**
- Rejected six times in the batch ("don't need 7th shot", "different setting we don't need").
- The "vary environment per shot" premise broke environment-lock, fought every other directive in the sequence.
- Filter logic in `shotsForCategory()` now excludes `s.n===7` for STANDARD/ADJUSTABLE.
- Shot 7 still exists in `_S` array (data preserved) — just not generated.
- New tier counts: **SIMPLE 5 / MID 7 / STANDARD 8 / ADJUSTABLE 10** (was 5 / 7 / 9 / 11).

---

## Shot 1 (Hero) — three new directives

1. **CROP SAFETY** block, mandatory on every Shot 1:
   - Product occupies central 65–75% of frame width
   - No element of product within 12% of any frame edge
   - Outer 15% on each side declared as a sacrifice zone for the website's 3:4 vertical crop
   - Replaces the old single-line "Product centered in frame for square and portrait crop safety"

2. **HERO ANGLE LOCK** for mechanism categories (Bouncer, Walker, Swing, Baby Swing, Baby Rocker, Recliner Chair, Play Gym):
   - Forces front three-quarter 45° view
   - "Never behind it, never to the rear, never showing the back panel as the primary subject"
   - New module-scope constant `MECHANISM_CATS` and `isMechanism()` helper

3. **ROOM GEOMETRY: STANDARD RECTANGULAR** for the same mechanism categories:
   - No arches, niches, vaulted ceilings, alcoves, bay windows
   - Targets the "no need for arches or coves" feedback on Baby Rocker

---

## Shot 8 (Model Lifestyle) — two new blocks

1. **MODESTY & REGION GUARDRAIL** — fully clothed, no nursing, no breastfeeding, no exposed skin beyond hands/neck/face. "Holding the baby comfortably is the maximum closeness."
2. **PROP CONTROL — STRICT** — max 1–2 contextual nursery props, explicit allow-list (soft toy, knit throw, board book, fabric basket) and deny-list (pampas grass, abstract framed art, decorative vases, plant arrangements, dining accessories).

---

## Shot 9 (Layered Luxe) — new dedicated block

Previously had only a one-line camera spec. Now:
- Explicit differentiation from Shot 8 (no model, no baby in frame)
- Same prop allow/deny list as Shot 8
- Maximum 2 small contextual props
- Targets "9th shot too many props", "last 2 shots are the same" feedback

---

## Shots 10 & 11 (Feature High / Feature Low) — strengthened

Added explicit **PROP CONTROL — ZERO PROPS** line:
- "No soft toys, no throws, no books, no folded items, no decorative props of any kind"
- "The composition is product-only against a clean uncluttered background"
- Targets "10th shot too many props" feedback for Crib and similar adjustable categories

---

## Negative prompt — global additions

Appended to every shot's negative block:
- "NO prop overload, NO cluttered styling, NO multiple decorative items competing with the product"

For non-T2 (editorial) shots, also added:
- "NO pampas grass, NO generic decorative vases, NO abstract framed wall art, NO dining accessories"

---

## Category data fixes (in fallback `_C` array)

**Feeding Pillow** interaction string changed:
- Before: `"Parent nursing with feeding pillow, parent positioning feeding pillow, baby resting on feeding pillow"`
- After: `"Parent seated holding baby comfortably with feeding pillow across lap, parent positioning feeding pillow on chair, baby resting on feeding pillow"`
- The first-string is what `split(",")[0]` injects into the model prompt.

**Recliner Chair** interaction string changed:
- Before: `"Parent seated in recliner holding baby, parent nursing in reclined position, parent reading in recliner with baby"`
- After: `"Parent seated in recliner holding baby, parent rocking gently with baby in recliner, parent reading in recliner with baby"`

⚠️ **These edits only affect the offline-fallback data.** The live app pulls from Supabase. To make these stick in production, run the same edits in Supabase `categories` table for those two rows. SQL/UI update is the next step (deferred for now, modesty guardrail in Shot 8 backs it up regardless).
