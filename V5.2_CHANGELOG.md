# V5.2 Patch — what changed

Built on top of V5.1. Driven by feedback from the Crib gen (cropping on left, pampas grass leaking) and the Bassinet gen (no bed in lifestyle).

---

## Shot 1 — centering and breathing-room rewrites

**Killed the "off-center" instruction.** The V5.1 large-product camera spec literally said "product placed off-center" — that was fighting crop safety. Replaced with explicit centering: product's vertical center axis aligns with image's vertical center axis, ±5% tolerance.

**Reframed "breathing room"** so the model can't reinterpret it as "fill the side with another piece of furniture." New language: *"The side margins of the frame are EMPTY environmental space — wall surface, floor, soft daylight, drapery — NOT space for other furniture, secondary product groupings, or styling vignettes. No second hero piece on the side opposite the product."*

**Tightened the sacrifice zone** from 15% to 18% on each side. Crop safety block now also explicitly states the crop trims ~12.5% per side, so the 18% margin is the safety buffer against that.

---

## Shot 8 — lifestyle scene context

**New `lifestyle_context` field on category data.** When present, gets injected into Shot 8 as a "LIFESTYLE SCENE CONTEXT — NON-NEGOTIABLE" block above the modesty guardrail.

Seeded so far:
- **Bassinet** → "Scene is set in a parent bedroom, NOT a nursery. The parent's bed is visible at the edge of the frame, partially cropped. The bassinet is positioned adjacent to the bed in a co-sleep configuration."
- **Carry Cot** → "Placed on the floor on a soft rug, OR on a low ottoman or bench at knee height — NEVER on a dining table, kitchen counter, desk, or any high adult-furniture surface. Parent is kneeling, crouching, or seated low."

Field is opt-in — categories without it generate Shot 8 as before.

---

## Pampas grass — removed at source

Was leaking through despite V5.1 negative prompt because it was in the *positive* aesthetic data. Removed from:
- Warm Tonal Nursery materials → replaced with "soft folded muslin blankets"
- Luxe Neutral Nursery materials → replaced with "soft folded knit textiles"

Negative prompt still reinforces (deny-list intact for any leak-through from Supabase data we haven't touched).

---

## Negative prompt — three additions

For non-T2 (editorial) shots, appended:
- "NO pendant lights or chandeliers hung directly above the product"
- "NO secondary furniture pieces filling the frame opposite the product"
- "NO product placed off-center or to one side of the frame"

The pendant rule is preventive, based on the Bouncer gen — wasn't broken yet, but it's a tendency in mechanism-category gens.

---

## What's NOT in V5.2 (still deferred)

- Per-category `surface_placement` for Wardrobe, Pillow, Feeding Pillow (still relying on Shot 1 generic placement)
- Prop pool split in Supabase
- `detail_focus_point` per category for Shot 5
- Cleaning the Supabase `categories` table directly (Bassinet/Carry Cot lifestyle_context only lives in fallback `_C` for now)

The Supabase work is still warranted, but only after we see what V5.2 alone fixes in the test sequences.

## Test prompt CSVs

`v52_test_prompts/` contains one CSV per category — 27 total — with one prompt sequence per file. Drop into your existing `generate.js` pipeline. Format matches the existing downloadCSV output exactly (`shot-N-prompt` headers, one row of quoted prompts).

Note: these CSVs were generated using the V5.2 fallback data (the `_A` / `_W` / `_L` / `_C` arrays embedded in `index.html`), not your live Supabase taxonomy. If your Supabase has been updated beyond the fallback, the production-fidelity path is to upload the patched index.html to GitHub Pages and use your live UI to download per-category. The deltas should be small — same logic, same rules, just whatever taxonomy edits you've made in Supabase since the fallback was last refreshed.
