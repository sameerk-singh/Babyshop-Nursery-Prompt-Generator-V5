# V5.3 Changelog — Aesthetic regression fix

## Problem identified

V5.2 batch testing showed regression on 7 of 15 hero shots versus V4-approved deck. Pattern: when certain aesthetics randomized into mechanism categories (Bouncer, Walker, Swing, Baby Rocker, Recliner, Play Gym, Changing Table), their *positive* materials descriptions dragged in architectural drama, secondary furniture, and problematic floors that overrode the late-stage mechanism rules and negative prompt.

Failing aesthetics across the V5.2 batch:
- **Mediterranean** → terracotta tile floors (Baby Rocker, Mattress)
- **Boho** → arched plaster niches (carry-over from earlier batches)
- **Cottagecore** → fringed canopies + 3 wreaths + canopied crib (Play Gym blowout)
- **Sunset Bedtime** → "white bed frame OR crib" inserts a bed/crib behind product (Baby Rocker, Walker)
- **Luxe Neutral** → tufted headboard + adult living-room vibe (Swing)
- **Boutique Editorial** → "premium architectural composition" → arched alcove (Changing Table, Feeding Pillow)
- **Storybook** → "white painted crib and wardrobe + linen-upholstered armchair" forces three pieces of secondary furniture into every shot

## V5.3 changes

### 1. Aesthetic data — surgical clean of `_A` array

Stripped prescriptive furniture / architectural directives from materials & mood fields, kept mood + palette + light quality. Each cleanup includes an explicit anti-directive in parentheses to suppress the AI prior.

| Aesthetic | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Boho | "...arched plaster niches, recessed shelving, terracotta accents" | "...recessed shelving, terracotta-accented small ceramics (no architectural niches, no arched alcoves)" |
| Mediterranean | "...terracotta-soft tile, artisanal wood, olive branches..." | "...soft warm plaster, artisanal wood furniture, olive branches as small accent (floor is wood plank or pale stone, NOT terracotta tile, NOT red tile)" |
| Boutique Editorial mood | "...sculptural, premium architectural composition" | "...sculptural, premium gallery composition with flat wall planes (no arches, no alcoves)" |
| Cottagecore | "...dried flower wreaths, fringed canopies, delicate lace, vintage wooden toys" | "...one small dried floral arrangement in jar (NOT wreaths, NOT canopies hung above the product, NOT multiple decorative items), delicate lace edge details" |
| Sunset Bedtime | "...white bed frame or crib, ...nightlight beside the bed..." | "...nightlight on the wall or wall-shelf, ...book rack OR small woven basket... (no secondary bed or crib introduced as decor — only the product itself sits in the scene)" |
| Luxe Neutral | "...marble or stone details, cashmere throws, tufted headboard accents..." | "...marble or stone accents, cashmere throws, soft folded knit textiles (no upholstered beds or tufted headboards introduced as decor)" |
| Storybook | "...white painted crib and wardrobe, linen-upholstered armchair with cream throw, fabric garland..." | "...fabric garland, soft pastel pink and cream textiles, simple rug on open center floor (no introduced crib, wardrobe, or armchair beyond the hero product)" |

### 2. Mechanism-safe aesthetic filter

Added `MECHANISM_SAFE_AES = {scandi, coastal, japandi, warmtona, genderne, colorpop, playful, boutique}`.

When a mechanism category is selected with random aesthetic mode, the random pick is restricted to this safe subset. Mediterranean / Boho / Cottagecore / Sunset / Luxe Neutral / Heritage / Storybook / Graphics are never randomly picked for mechanism categories — they remain available via manual selection if you want to test them deliberately.

Verified in V5.3 batch generation: all 8 mechanism categories drew from the safe subset.

### 3. Changing Table added to MECHANISM_CATS

V5.2 batch produced an arched plaster alcove around the Changing Table because it wasn't in `MECHANISM_CATS`. V5.3 includes it, so it now gets the standard rectangular room geometry rule and the mechanism-safe aesthetic filter.

### 4. Negative prompt — six new specific denials

Added to the per-shot negative block:

```
NO terracotta tile floor, NO red brick floor, NO decorative wreaths on walls,
NO canopied alcoves or fabric canopies hung over the product,
NO plaster archways framing the product,
NO secondary hero-scale furniture (chairs, cribs, beds, wardrobes, dressers)
  competing with the product as a second visual subject
  (small accent items like baskets, stools, soft toys, and books are fine).
```

Note the explicit allow-list at the end — accent items (small baskets, stools, plushies, books) are fine because they appear in the V4 approved deck and don't compete with the hero product. The denial is specifically against second hero-scale furniture pieces.

### 5. Storage category added

Was missing from V5.2 fallback `_C` array. Added with 8-shot count (medium size, non-adjustable, non-mechanism).

### 6. Version bump

V5.2 → V5.3 in title, login splash, header.
